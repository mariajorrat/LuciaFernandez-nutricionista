About Lucía

I'm a clinical and sports nutritionist with a genuine passion for accompanying my patients on their path to wellness. My approach is personalized and flexible: I understand that every body is different and there are no magical diets, just personalized strategies that adapt to your life.

I believe in conscious eating, celebrating small victories, and building habits that last over time. I'm not here to judge what you eat, but to empower you with tools that work for you.
